# Client Acquisition System

You are an expert at building systematic, repeatable client acquisition for a B2B services business. This skill ties together prospecting, outreach, nurture, and closing into one end-to-end playbook specifically for selling talent sourcing, lead generation, and event activation services to SaaS companies Series A through D.

## Your Business at a Glance

**Service 1 — Talent Sourcing**
Finding hard-to-find candidates for high-growth SaaS companies
Target buyer: CEO, COO, VP People, Head of Talent
Price signal: $2,000-8,000/month retainer + placement fees

**Service 2 — AI-Powered Lead Gen & Outreach**
Email + LinkedIn automation to generate qualified pipeline
Target buyer: CEO, VP Sales, Head of Growth, CRO
Price signal: $1,500-6,000/month retainer

**Service 3 — Event Activation**
Converting event attendance into booked meetings and pipeline
Target buyer: VP Marketing, Head of Demand Gen, CEO
Price signal: $3,000-15,000 per event

## The Acquisition Engine (Weekly System)

### Monday — Prospecting (90 min)
```
1. Check Crunchbase for new funding announcements (30 min)
   → Add funded SaaS companies to your target list
   → Note: stage, size, what they do

2. Check LinkedIn for trigger events (30 min)
   → Search: "We're hiring" + SaaS + Series B/C
   → Check followed companies for job posting spikes

3. Build this week's outreach list (30 min)
   → 20 new Tier 1 targets
   → Score each against ICP
   → Find decision maker + contact info
```

### Tuesday-Thursday — Outreach (60 min/day)
```
Morning (30 min):
→ Send 5-10 personalized cold emails
→ Send 5-7 LinkedIn connection requests
→ Follow up on pending conversations (Day 3/7/14 touches)

Afternoon (30 min):
→ Engage with prospects' LinkedIn content (5-10 comments)
→ Check replies and respond within 2 hours
→ Book any interested prospects into calendar
```

### Friday — Review & Nurture (60 min)
```
→ Update CRM / tracking sheet
→ Move prospects through pipeline stages
→ Send weekly value-add to warm prospects
→ Review metrics for the week
→ Plan next week's target list
```

## Pipeline Stages & Actions

```
STAGE 1: PROSPECT
Definition: In your ICP, contact info found, not yet contacted
Action: Add to weekly outreach sequence
Target: 100+ prospects in this stage at all times

STAGE 2: CONTACTED
Definition: First outreach sent, no reply yet
Action: Follow up sequence (Day 3, 7, 14, 21)
Target: 50+ prospects in this stage

STAGE 3: ENGAGED
Definition: Replied, showed interest, conversation started
Action: Book discovery call within 48 hours
Target: 10-20 prospects in this stage

STAGE 4: DISCOVERY CALL BOOKED
Definition: Call scheduled
Action: Prepare, research, send confirmation
Target: 5-10 calls per month

STAGE 5: PROPOSAL SENT
Definition: Discovery call done, proposal delivered
Action: Follow up within 48 hours, offer to walk through together
Target: 3-6 proposals active at any time

STAGE 6: NEGOTIATION
Definition: They're interested, discussing terms
Action: Address objections, adjust scope if needed
Target: 1-3 deals in this stage

STAGE 7: CLOSED WON
Definition: Contract signed, payment received
Action: Trigger onboarding sequence immediately
Target: 2-4 new clients per month (depending on capacity)

STAGE 8: CLOSED LOST
Definition: Not moving forward
Action: Note reason, add to nurture, ask for referral
```

## Referral System

Your fastest, cheapest client acquisition channel.

### Who to Ask
- Happy current clients (after first win)
- Past clients (even ones you haven't spoken to in months)
- People who said "not right now" (they know others who need you)
- VC/investor contacts (they know all their portfolio companies)
- SaaS founders you've met at events

### The Ask
```
"We're really proud of the results we've achieved together.

Our best clients usually come through referrals — do you know 
1-2 other SaaS founders or HR/sales leaders who might be 
dealing with similar challenges?

I'd make sure they get the same level of attention we give you."
```

### Partner Channels
- **VCs and accelerators** — they want their portfolio to succeed
  → Offer: Free 30-min talent/GTM audit for their portfolio companies
  → Ask: Intro to portfolio companies that are hiring or scaling GTM

- **SaaS tools** (Salesforce, HubSpot, LinkedIn partners)
  → Co-market to their customer base
  → Offer referral fee for closed clients

- **Complementary services** (PR, branding, SaaS consultants)
  → They have the same clients, different services
  → Cross-refer mutually

## The 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30 — Foundation
- Build ICP and target list (200+ prospects)
- Set up email domain + warm up
- Create LinkedIn content calendar
- Write and test 3 outreach sequences
- Launch outreach (start with 10 emails/day)
- Goal: First 3 discovery calls

### Days 31-60 — Momentum
- Scale outreach to 30-50 emails/day
- Optimize sequences based on reply data
- Launch LinkedIn content (3x/week)
- Convert first discovery calls to proposals
- Goal: First 2 clients signed

### Days 61-90 — Scale
- Referral system activated with first clients
- Partner outreach to VCs + accelerators
- Scale what's working, cut what isn't
- Build case studies from first clients
- Goal: 4-6 active clients, predictable pipeline

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- **Chasing everyone** — 20 perfect prospects beat 200 random ones
- **Pitching on first contact** — earn the right to present
- **No follow-up system** — 80% of deals close after the 5th touch
- **Ignoring referrals** — your happiest clients are your best salespeople
- **No tracking** — if you don't measure it, you can't improve it
- **Taking bad-fit clients** — they drain energy and generate no referrals
- **Stopping outreach when busy** — pipeline takes 30-60 days to convert
